Hello!
I'm Michael.

I am a PhD student in the African Cultural Studies Department in the University of Wisconsin at Madison. I write and think about the numerous places where language and culture meet in everyday life situations and try to make sense of how that interaction informs the fascinating ways in which identities are constantly reworked and behaviors are repetitively learned, unlearned, and relearned.

Head on to my Bio page to learn more about me. Make sure to visit my Teaching page to read about my teaching.
