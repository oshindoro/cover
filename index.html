Michael Ọ̀shíndọrọ̀.

Michael is a first-year PhD student in the African Cultural Studies program at University of Madison-Wisconsin. His academic interests span different areas of language and culture and how these topics interact to influence identities—gender, racial, ethnic... He is currently working on how African cultural practices and expressions of identity are re-enacted in African animated films.

In his spare time, he is either listening to comedy or learning to code (the result of which is this website).
